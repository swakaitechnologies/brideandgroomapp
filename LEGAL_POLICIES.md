# 3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

## 3.1 General Refund Policy

All premium subscription purchases on the Bride & Groom platform are for digital services and are generally **non-refundable** once the subscription is activated and the user has accessed premium features.

This policy is designed in compliance with the **Consumer Protection Act, 2019**, **Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020**, and the requirements of our payment gateway partners (Razorpay and Stripe).

## 3.2 Eligible Refund Scenarios

We will consider refund requests in the following exceptional situations:

| Scenario | Refund Type | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **Duplicate Payment:** You were charged twice for the same subscription due to a technical error. | **Full Refund** of the duplicate charge | Within 5–7 business days |
| **Payment Deducted but Subscription Not Activated:** Amount was debited from your account but the subscription was not activated in the App. | **Full Refund** | Within 5–7 business days |
| **Technical Inability to Use Service:** A verifiable technical issue on our end prevented you from using any premium features for the entire subscription duration. | **Full or Prorated Refund** (at our discretion) | Within 7–10 business days |
| **Within 48-Hour Cooling-Off Period:** You request a refund within 48 hours of purchase AND you have NOT used any premium features (no contacts viewed, no messages sent, no calls made). | **Full Refund** | Within 5–7 business days |
| **Account Banned Due to Moderation Error:** Your account was suspended in error and later reinstated by our moderation team. | **Prorated Refund or Extension** for the lost subscription days | Within 7–10 business days |

## 3.3 Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

- You have already used premium features (viewed contacts, sent messages, or made calls) beyond the 48-hour cooling-off period.
- You found a matrimonial partner during the subscription period and no longer wish to use the Platform.
- You changed your mind about the subscription after the 48-hour cooling-off period.
- Your account was suspended or terminated for violating the Terms and Conditions or Community Guidelines.
- You claim dissatisfaction with the quality or quantity of profiles available on the Platform.
- You did not use the subscription during its validity period (non-usage does not constitute grounds for a refund).
- The subscription was purchased using a promotional discount or coupon code (unless the specific promotion states otherwise).
- Auto-renewed subscriptions where the user failed to disable auto-renewal before the renewal date (however, if unused, we may offer a courtesy refund at our discretion).

## 3.4 How to Request a Refund

1. **In-App:** Navigate to **Account Settings → Help & Support → Submit a Query** and select "Payment / Refund Issue" as the category.
2. **Email:** Send an email to **support@brideandgroom.co.in** with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Profile ID]". Include:
   - Your registered email address and mobile number.
   - Your Matrimonial Profile ID (e.g., EM-XXXXXX).
   - The date and amount of the transaction.
   - The payment gateway transaction/order ID (if available).
   - A clear description of the reason for the refund request.
3. **Response Time:** We will acknowledge your request within **24 hours** and provide a resolution within **5 business days**.

## 3.5 Refund Processing

- Approved refunds will be credited to the **original payment method** (bank account, credit card, debit card, or UPI) used at the time of purchase.
- Refunds processed via Razorpay are typically credited within **5–7 business days**.
- Refunds processed via Stripe are typically credited within **5–10 business days**.
- The exact timeline may vary depending on your bank or financial institution.
- You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is initiated.

## 3.6 Subscription Cancellation

- You may cancel your active subscription at any time from **Account Settings → Subscription**.
- Upon cancellation, your premium features will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
- After the billing period ends, your account will revert to the free plan.
- Cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for the remaining unused days of the current billing period.
- If auto-renewal is enabled, cancellation will also disable auto-renewal.

## 3.7 Plan Changes & Upgrades

- You may upgrade to a higher-tier plan at any time. The prorated remaining value of your current plan will be applied as a discount to the new plan.
- Downgrading to a lower-tier plan is not supported mid-cycle. You may switch to a lower plan when your current plan expires.

## 3.8 Razorpay & Stripe Compliance

As per the refund policies mandated by our payment gateway partners:

- **Razorpay:** Refunds are processed through the Razorpay Refund API. Full and partial refunds are supported. All refund transactions are recorded with a unique Refund ID and timestamp.

# 4. Cookie Policy

## 4.1 What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device by a web browser. They enable the Platform to recognize your device, maintain session state, and remember your preferences.

## 4.2 Cookies We Use

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Type | Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|
| `token` | Authentication — Maintains your logged-in session | Essential / HTTP-only | 24 hours |
| `refreshToken` | Session Refresh — Issues new access tokens without re-login | Essential / HTTP-only | 7 days |

## 4.3 Mobile Application

The Bride & Groom mobile application uses **encrypted device storage** (EncryptedStorage / AsyncStorage) instead of traditional browser cookies for authentication tokens. These tokens are stored locally on your device and are not accessible by third-party applications.

## 4.4 Your Choices

- Essential cookies are required for the Platform to function and cannot be disabled.
- We do NOT use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.
- You may clear cookies from your browser settings at any time, but this may require you to log in again.
